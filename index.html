Canva Embed Setup Guide

  1. Configure your wedding title, time offset, styles, and typography.
  2. Click Generate Canva Embed Link below the customizer.
  3. Copy the generated custom widget URL.
  4. Important: Ensure you copy the generated URL from the text block. Do not paste the base editor URL into Canva.
  5. In Canva, go to Apps → search Embeds → Paste your link.
  6. Resize the iframe box container: it responds dynamically without borders or background clipping.
